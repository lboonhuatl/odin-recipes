Description

This is a picture of ginger chicken cooked with delicious white chicken and ginger. This dish originated from China.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Shaoxing wine or rice vinegar
  • White and black pepper
  • Water
  • scallions
  • Olive oil
  • Skinless chicken
  • Kosher salt
  • Fresh ginger
  • Chopped garlic
  • Cornstarch
  • Garnish
  • white rice

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Whisk sugar, soy sauce, black and white pepper together
  3. Heat oil and put in the chicken
  4. Add ginger and garlic
  5. Whisk cornstarch together
  6. Put in the chicken and let it rest
