Description

This is a picture of lasagna which is a pasta made out of wide, flat sheets. This dish originated from Italy.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna accordingly
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
